ClawHQ vs LangSmith

LangSmith traces and evaluates LLM calls. ClawHQ deploys and operates a full AI agent team. They solve different problems — here's when each is the right fit.

ClawHQ

AI agent deployment platform

30+ agents pre-installed and ready to run. One Docker command deploys your entire AI team — wired to Discord, Slack, and Telegram. Built for teams who want agents in production today, not after weeks of building.

LangSmith

LLM observability and evaluation

Tracing, dataset management, evaluation runs, and human annotations for LLM applications. Built by LangChain, optimized for teams building with LangChain. Best for debugging and improving LLM quality over time.

Choose ClawHQ when you want to...

  • Deploy a multi-agent AI team without building agents from scratch
  • Operate agents in production with permissions and budget controls
  • Get a full audit trail of every agent action
  • Manage agents across Discord, Slack, and Telegram
  • Extend capabilities through the ClawMart skill pack marketplace

Choose LangSmith when you want to...

  • Debug LLM calls with full trace trees during development
  • Run automated evaluations against test datasets
  • Version and A/B test prompts across releases
  • Annotate LLM outputs with human feedback
  • Monitor LLM quality metrics over time
