Capabilities

Everything your agents need.

Multi-Provider LLMs

Every frontier model. One interface.

Switch providers per-agent or per-task. Route cheap tasks to fast models, complex reasoning to the frontier.

Anthropic OpenAI Groq OpenRouter Ollama Gemini Mistral + more

Persistent Memory

Agents that remember.

Vector + episodic memory. Agents recall past conversations, learn preferences, and build context over time.

Workflow Automation

Scheduled. Triggered. Automated.

Cron jobs, event triggers, and multi-step pipelines. Your agents work even while you sleep.

Cross-Platform Channels
