ClawHQ vs Dify

Dify is a visual LLM app builder. ClawHQ ships with a complete AI team ready to deploy. Different tools for different goals.

ClawHQ

Pre-deployed AI team

30+ agents live immediately after one Docker command. Wired to Discord, Slack, and Telegram. You delegate tasks — the team coordinates. Best when you want results without building pipelines.

Dify

Visual LLM app builder

Drag-and-drop editor for constructing AI workflows, chatbots, and agents from scratch. Full control over every node and flow. Best when you want to design exactly how your AI behaves.

Choose ClawHQ when you want to...

  • Have a full AI team operational without building anything
  • Delegate tasks through Discord, Slack, or Telegram immediately
  • Deploy a coordinated multi-agent system in one command

  • Extend with skill packs from the ClawMart community registry

  • Design custom AI workflows and chatbots with a visual editor
  • Full control over exactly how each agent behaves at every step

  • Create user-facing chatbot interfaces without code
